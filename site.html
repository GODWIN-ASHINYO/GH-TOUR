Osu Castle, also known as Fort Christiansborg, is a historic coastal fortress in Accra that played roles in the trans-Atlantic slave trade and later served as Ghana’s seat of government during periods of national history..

Independence Arch is a national monument located at Black Star Square in Accra, symbolizing Ghana’s freedom, independence, and the struggles of leaders who fought against colonial rule to achieve self-governance and national pride for citizens..
