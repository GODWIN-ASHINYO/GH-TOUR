TOUR GH

About Tour GH Tour GH is a tourism and travel support company dedicated to promoting Ghana’s rich cultural heritage, historical landmarks, and natural attractions. The company was established to provide tourists with reliable guidance and quality services that enhance their travel experience across Ghana. Tour GH focuses on connecting visitors to popular tourist destinations, cultural sites, and recreational centers while ensuring comfort, safety, and satisfaction. The company works with trained tour guides, hospitality partners, and local communities to deliver informative, enjoyable, and well-organized tour experiences. Through its services, Tour GH aims to support the growth of Ghana’s tourism industry by encouraging local and international tourism. The company is committed to professionalism, customer satisfaction, and the promotion of Ghana as a peaceful, welcoming, and culturally rich destination.

PURPOSE & FUNCTIONS

  • Provides information on major tourist sites in Ghana
  • Shows the locations of tourist attractions across the country
  • Displays pictures and videos of tourist sites
  • Shares opening and closing hours of tourist centers
  • Allows visitors to learn about booking options
  • Provides information about services and nearby accommodations
