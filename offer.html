Meet Our Team

This project was developed by a committed team of students with a shared interest in promoting tourism in Ghana. The team worked collaboratively to research, design, and develop a website that provides accurate and useful information about tourist sites across the country. Each member contributed ideas and skills to ensure the project met academic requirements. Throughout the project, team members took responsibility for different tasks such as content research, webpage design, and website development. Effective communication and teamwork helped overcome challenges and ensured the successful completion of the website. The team aims to support tourism awareness and showcase Ghana’s cultural and historical heritage through this project.
